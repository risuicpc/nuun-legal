Account Deletion Instructions

For Nuun - Business Management Application

Last Updated: October 2025

This page explains how to permanently delete your account and all associated data from Nuun. Account deletion is immediate and irreversible.

⚠️ Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, all your data will be immediately and permanently removed from our systems. You will not be able to recover any information.

How to Delete Your Account

1 Open the App and Go to Profile

Launch Nuun and log in to your account. Tap on the Profile tab at the bottom right corner of the screen.

2 Navigate to Account Settings

In the Profile section, scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option under the Settings. Tap on it to proceed.

3 Read the Warning Screen

You will see a detailed warning explaining what data will be deleted. Read this carefully. Tap "I Understand, Continue" to proceed to the confirmation screen.

4 Confirm Your Identity

On the confirmation screen, you will need to:

  • Type "DELETE MY ACCOUNT" exactly as shown
  • Enter your current password for verification
5 Final Confirmation

Tap "Permanently Delete Account". A final confirmation alert will appear. Tap "Yes, Delete Everything" to immediately and permanently delete your account.

6 Automatic Logout

After successful deletion, you will be automatically logged out and redirected to the login screen. Your account and all data have been permanently removed.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is immediately and permanently deleted:

Personal Information
  • Your profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Account credentials and login information
  • User preferences and settings
Business Data
  • All businesses you own (including business names, types, descriptions)
  • Business team members and their roles
  • All business-related activities and transactions
App Data
  • Your activity history within the app
  • Any stored preferences or customizations
  • Analytics data associated with your account

Data Retention Period

Immediate Deletion: All your personal data and business information are immediately and permanently deleted when you confirm account deletion. There is no retention period for user-requested deletions.

What Happens to Your Businesses

If you are the owner of any businesses:

  • All businesses you own will be permanently deleted
  • All team members will lose access to these businesses
  • All business data will be immediately removed

If you are a team member in other businesses:

  • You will be removed from all businesses where you are a team member
  • The businesses themselves will continue to operate normally
  • Business owners will be notified of your removal
🔒 Data Security Note

We take your privacy seriously. When you delete your account, we use secure data erasure methods to ensure your information cannot be recovered. Our deletion process complies with data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.
